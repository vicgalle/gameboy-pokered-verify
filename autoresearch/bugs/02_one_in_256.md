# Bug: 1/256 Miss Chance on "100% Accuracy" Moves

## Gameplay Description

In Pokemon Red/Blue, moves that are supposed to have 100% accuracy (like Swift or
most Normal-type moves at full accuracy) can still miss with a probability of
approximately 1 in 256. This means that even guaranteed-hit moves occasionally fail.

The same pattern also affects critical hit calculations — there is a 1/256 chance
that the maximum possible critical hit rate still fails to produce a critical hit.
