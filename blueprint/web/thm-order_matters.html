12 Accuracy/Evasion Non-Cancellation

The two-pass hit chance calculation uses truncated table fractions and intermediate floor division. Equal \(\pm \) boosts do not cancel.

[CalcHitChance] PokeredBugs.calcHitChance Two-pass calculation: accuracy stage first, then evasion stage, with integer truncation.

[+1 Reduces Accuracy] PokeredBugs.plus1_reduces_accuracy def:calc_hit_chance Equal \(\pm 1\) boosts on a 255-accuracy move give 252 instead of 255.

[+5 Worst Non-Cancellation] PokeredBugs.plus5_worst_noncancellation def:calc_hit_chance Equal \(\pm 5\) boosts lose 6 accuracy points: 249 instead of 255.

[Non-Cancellation Table] PokeredBugs.noncancellation_table def:calc_hit_chance Complete characterization: only \(\pm 0\), \(\pm 2\), \(\pm 6\) perfectly cancel.

[Ratio Product Not One] PokeredBugs.ratio_product_not_one Root cause: \(15/10 \times 66/100 = 990/1000 \neq 1\).

[Order Matters] PokeredBugs.order_matters def:calc_hit_chance Reversing the pass order gives different results (non-commutative).
