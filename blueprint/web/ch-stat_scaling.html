9 Stat Scaling Bugs

When stats exceed 255, both are divided by 4 and truncated to 8 bits. Defense can reach 0 (freeze), and values \({\gt} 1023\) wrap catastrophically.

9.1 Definitions

Definition 9.1 Scale Div 4
✓
#

Lean declarations

  • PokeredBugs.scaleDiv4

\(\texttt{stat} / 4 \bmod 256\). Models two srl/rr pairs keeping only the low byte.

Definition 9.2 Stat Scaling
✓
#

Uses

  • Definition 9.1

Lean declarations

  • PokeredBugs.statScaling

Full scaling routine: both stats divided by 4 when either \({\gt} 255\). Attack bumped to 1 if zero; defense is not.
