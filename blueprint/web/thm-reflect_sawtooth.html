10 Reflect/Light Screen Overflow

Reflect doubles defense with no cap. When defense \(\ge 512\), the doubled value exceeds 1023 and wraps in .scaleStats—reducing defense instead of doubling it.

[Effective Defense] PokeredBugs.effectiveDefense def:scale_div4 Defense after optional Reflect, then scaling. Returns 0 on freeze.

[Reflect 512 Freezes] PokeredBugs.reflect_512_freezes def:effective_defense Defense 512 with Reflect: \(1024/4 = 256 \bmod 256 = 0\). Game freeze.

[Reflect Hurts Cloyster] PokeredBugs.reflect_hurts_cloyster def:effective_defense Cloyster with Barrier + Reflect: effective defense drops from 174 to 92.

[Reflect Helps Below 512] PokeredBugs.reflect_helps_below_512 def:effective_defense Below 512, Reflect always increases effective defense.

[Reflect Hurts Above 512] PokeredBugs.reflect_hurts_above_512 def:effective_defense Above 512, Reflect always decreases effective defense.

[Reflect Sawtooth] PokeredBugs.reflect_sawtooth def:effective_defense Full sawtooth pattern: peaks at 511 (255), crashes to 0 at 512, slowly recovers.
